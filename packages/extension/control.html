Capture

A screen recording captures whatever you pick in Chrome's share dialog, including windows from other apps and a second monitor. It has no page structure, so element zooms, drift checks and regeneration are unavailable.

Matched elements are blurred in the editor and in everything it exports. The recorded video itself keeps the original pixels.
